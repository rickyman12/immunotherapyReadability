SITC Cancer Immunotherapy CONNECT



Making Cure a Reality

It is the mission of the Society for Immunotherapy of Cancer (SITC) to improve
cancer patient outcomes by advancing the science, development and application
of cancer immunology and immunotherapy through our core values of
interaction/integration, innovation, translation and leadership in the field.
SITC aims to make cancer immunotherapy a standard of care and the word “cure”
a reality for cancer patients everywhere.
